Biography

I am currently an associate professor in the Department of Applied Statistics at Beijing Technology and Business University. I obtained my PhD in the School of Computer Science at Guangdong University of Technology (2017 - 2020), supervised by Prof Ruichu Cai and co-supervised by Prof Kun Zhang (Carnegie Mellon University).
